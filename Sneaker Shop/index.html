Nike Blazer

The Nike Blazer is one of the first basketball sneakers from Nike, first introduced in 1973. A legendary model with a large "Swoosh", who found herself not only on the parquet, but also in skateboarding. A well-deserved classic, without which it is impossible to imagine Nike. The Sneaker Encyclopedia of the Streer Beat chain of stores tells about the success of sneakers in the 70s, skateboarding, and the current popularity of the Nike Blazer.

Founded by Ken Block and Damon Way in 1993, DC Shoes has rapidly grown to become one of the industry leaders in the production of skateboarding shoes and extreme sports clothing.
